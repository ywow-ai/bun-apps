## What is WhoDidnt?

WhoDidnt analyzes two datasets:

- Accounts you **follow** (following)
- Accounts that **follow you** (followers)

By comparing these lists, the tool highlights differences and relationships between them.

---

## What can it show?

With WhoDidnt, you can identify:

- Accounts that **do not follow you back**
- Accounts that **mutually follow each other**
- The overall gap between followers and following

This removes the need to manually check profiles one by one.
